Hello, I'm Inês Branco

I'm an Illustrator and Graphic Designer

I hold a degree in Art and Design from ESE Coimbra and have experience working both freelance and in-house, along with participating in independent publications and collective exhibitions.
I'm comfortable working with tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. Procreate is a close companion in my creative process, and I can also manage a bit of HTML and CSS when needed.
Based in Porto, Portugal.
