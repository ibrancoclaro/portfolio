Inês Branco

Illustrator and Graphic Designer

Based in Porto, Portugal.
My art journey started early—drawing on the walls of my parents’ home. (They let me keep the colored pencils).
In 2018, I completed a bachelor's degree in Art and Design at ESE Coimbra. Since then, I’ve worked as a freelance illustrator and graphic designer, participating in zines and exhibitions, and selling at markets.
I enjoy playing with textures, writing stories, and adding a touch of something magical to my art.
